Living Style guides

What is a living style guide

Living style guides exist to make the life of a developer easy. They consist of certain fixed designs for you to use in the making of a webpage e.g. button layout and/or structural settings. A style guide will therefore consist of class definitions to be used throughout a project.

Why is a living style guide useful to developers and designers?

A living style guide will make the code and design much more reusable and flexible. In case of change in designer or in the need of updating/expanding layout, a well-documented style guide consisting all the needed designs and guidelines will make the work more foreseeable and maintain the style without too much work.

My list of living style guide tips

  1. Start by defining the most used components and structural layouts
  2. Always keep flexibility and maintainability in mind
  3. KISS: keeping it simple will make the guide easier to use
